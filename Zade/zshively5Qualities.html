5 Qualities

You're probably wondering what are the 5 Qualities, So the five qualities are Inquiry, Persistence, collaboration,
communication, and creativity.

The quality I have grown most in this year is Communication because I have communicated with alot of people this
year and have given insight into me more with teachers and others.
