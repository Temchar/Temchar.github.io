No Man's Sky

This is a great game. I highly recommend it. I haven't finished it yet. However, here's the basics of what it is: You are in space and there are a
vast amount of galaxies. It's a sandbox game which means you don't particularly have to do the story you can just mess around and explore. Maybe do
a few quests here and there. Whatever you want as long as you can do it in the game. You can also fight bandits and choose who you want to be. For
example whether you want to befriend this alien race or be enemy's with them. This game is all about exploring these galaxies and new worlds.
