P.B.L.

PBL, Project Based Learning, is essential at DRSS. This is because we love it and it
helps us learn better. Instead of having lectures
upon lectures upon lectures,and getting bored and not learning a single thing,
we have PBL where we have hands on activities so we can do it
and hopefully know it by the end of the activity.
